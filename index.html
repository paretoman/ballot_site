Smart Voting Simulator

An Explorable Guide to Group Decision Making

By Paretoman and Contributors, May 2020

Based off Work by Nicky Case

This is the home of the Smart Voting Simulator, a great resource to learn how voting methods work, visually and interactively.

You’ll really enjoy this site if you feel like there’s got to be a smarter way to vote than what we’re doing right now.

Intro

The Basics - #1 - Start here. See the 2D model we use for this site.
Finding Common Ground - #2 - Why do we vote? To make smarter group decisions.

In Depth

Condorcet Methods - the best way to find common ground
Approval Voting - a practical improvement - And it works with strategy.
STAR Voting - and a deeper dive into using strategy and the game of chicken.
Instant Runoff Voting - a kinder politics with a kind of Ranked Choice Voting (RCV) for single-winner elections
The Single Transferable Vote - an introduction to proportional representation with STV, the multi-winner form of Ranked Choice Voting RCV
Proportional Representation - Bring the candidates closer to the voters. Continuing after STV, here are more kinds of proportional representation without parties, using scoring and paired methods.
Primaries - They don't always work.
